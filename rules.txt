               
               [How To Play Wordle]

[1]You can have only 6 guesses to find out the correct word.
[2]You can only guess 5-letter words.
[3]Green - You guessed a letter in word at correct position.
[5]Yellow- You guessed a letter in word but the position is wrong.
[6]'-'(dash) - Correct word doesn't contain the letter.-
